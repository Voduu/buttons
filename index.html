Buttons Is a Cat

About Buttons:
 Buttons is a cat. He was first found wandering the wilderness in 20XX with his younger brother. One day he found some humans and took a liking to them. Unfortunately, his brother was much more skeptical and did not approach the creatures. Instead, he left the lands to Buttons and went in search of his own country to rule over.

Over time, Buttons came to be the overlord of these lands, in which he would one day wage a great war with the other cats of the nation. These wars earned him the battle scars he now posseses, including his damaged ear. As a result of the conflicts, other cats now fear Buttons and cower before him, or bring him tribute in the form of gifts or meals.

Buttons now spends his time leisurely strolling through the lands he worked tirelessly to protect along with the cats whom he deems worthy of his presence.
